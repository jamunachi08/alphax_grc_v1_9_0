# AlphaX GRC Process Manual

## Core Governance Cycle
1. Identify obligations, controls, assets, and owners.
2. Assess gaps, maturity, and risk.
3. Issue findings, remediation, and exceptions.
4. Monitor evidence, policy due dates, and KRIs.
5. Report to management and board.

## Audit Process
- Planning: objectives, scope, criteria, sample plan, timeline.
- Fieldwork: interviews, walkthroughs, evidence review, sampling.
- Reporting: findings, recommendations, management response.
- Follow-up: remediation verification and closure.

## Privacy Process
- Register processing activities.
- Track lawful basis, retention, disclosures, and requests.
- Link privacy risks and obligations to evidence and controls.

## Third-Party Process
- Classify vendor criticality.
- Run due diligence and security/privacy questionnaires.
- Track contract obligations, incidents, and periodic reassessment.
